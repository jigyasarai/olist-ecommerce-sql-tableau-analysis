# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

The analysis of the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset revealed strong revenue growth, high customer satisfaction, and significant differences in product performance across categories and states.

---

## Sales Performance Insights

### 1. Revenue Growth
- Total revenue exceeded **R$16 million**.
- Revenue showed steady growth throughout 2017 and peaked during 2018.
- The business experienced rapid expansion during the observed period.

### 2. State Contribution
- São Paulo (SP) generated the highest revenue.
- Revenue was concentrated in a small number of states.
- RJ and MG were the next strongest contributors.

### 3. Product Category Performance
- **beleza_saude** and **relogios_presentes** generated substantial revenue.
- Revenue contribution was concentrated among a few top-performing categories.

---

## Customer & Operations Insights

### 1. Customer Satisfaction
- Average review score remained above **4.0**.
- Most customers reported positive shopping experiences.

### 2. Delivery Performance
- On-time delivery rate reached **91.89%**.
- Operational performance was consistently strong.

### 3. Shipping Efficiency
- Average shipping duration was **12.5 days**.
- Faster delivery may further improve customer satisfaction.

---

## Product Insights

### 1. Top-Selling Categories
- **cama_mesa_banho** recorded the highest units sold.
- Several home and lifestyle categories dominated sales volume.

### 2. Highest Rated Categories
- Categories with more than 250 reviews maintained ratings above **4.0**.
- High customer ratings indicate strong product quality and customer experience.

### 3. Regional Category Dominance
- Different states exhibited different category preferences.
- Understanding regional demand can support targeted marketing strategies.

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase focus on high-revenue states such as São Paulo.
2. Expand inventory for top-selling categories.
3. Maintain current delivery performance standards.
4. Analyze highly rated categories for best practices that can be applied elsewhere.
5. Develop region-specific marketing campaigns based on category preferences.

---

## Conclusion

The Olist marketplace demonstrates strong sales growth, high customer satisfaction, and efficient operations. Strategic focus on top-performing states and categories can further improve revenue and customer retention.
