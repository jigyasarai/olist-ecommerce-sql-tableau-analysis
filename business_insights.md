Business Insights
Executive Summary
This analysis was conducted on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to evaluate sales performance, customer satisfaction, product demand, and operational efficiency.
The study identified key revenue drivers, regional purchasing patterns, customer experience metrics, and category-level performance indicators.

Sales Performance Insights
Total Revenue
	•	The platform generated approximately R$16 million in revenue.
	•	Revenue growth accelerated significantly during 2017 and 2018.
Order Volume
	•	The marketplace processed 99,441 unique customer orders.
	•	Average Order Value (AOV) was approximately R$160.99.
Customer Retention
	•	Repeat Customer Rate was approximately 3.12%.
	•	Most customers made only a single purchase, indicating opportunities for customer retention programs.

Geographic Insights
Revenue Distribution
	•	São Paulo (SP) generated the highest revenue among all Brazilian states.
	•	A small group of states contributed a significant share of total marketplace revenue.
Category Preferences by State
	•	Customer preferences varied across states.
	•	Different regions showed dominance in different product categories.
	•	Categories such as cama_mesa_banho, beleza_saude, and relogios_presentes frequently appeared as top-performing categories.
These findings suggest opportunities for region-specific marketing and inventory strategies.

Product Performance Insights
Top-Selling Categories
	•	cama_mesa_banho emerged as one of the strongest categories in terms of unit sales.
	•	Categories related to home improvement, beauty, and lifestyle products consistently performed well.
Category Demand
	•	Product demand was not evenly distributed across categories.
	•	A small number of categories generated a large proportion of total sales volume.

Customer Satisfaction Insights
Review Performance
	•	Average Review Score was approximately 4.09 / 5.
	•	Overall customer satisfaction remained high.
Highly Rated Categories
	•	Categories with more than 250 reviews maintained strong average ratings.
	•	High ratings combined with large review volumes indicate sustained customer satisfaction rather than isolated positive feedback.

Operational Insights
Delivery Performance
	•	Approximately 91.89% of orders were delivered on time.
	•	Delivery operations demonstrated strong reliability.
Shipping Efficiency
	•	Average shipping time was approximately 12.5 days.
	•	Delivery performance remained consistent despite large order volumes.
Seller Ecosystem
	•	The marketplace operated with 289 active sellers.
	•	Revenue generation was concentrated among a relatively small number of sellers and product categories.

Recommendations
Customer Retention
	•	Introduce loyalty programs and targeted promotions to improve repeat purchase rates.
Regional Marketing
	•	Customize promotions based on state-level category preferences.
Product Strategy
	•	Prioritize inventory and marketing investments in high-performing categories.
Customer Experience
	•	Continue monitoring review scores and delivery performance to maintain customer satisfaction.
Growth Opportunities
	•	Expand successful categories into regions where they are currently underrepresented.
